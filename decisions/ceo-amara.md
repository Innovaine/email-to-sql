# CEO greenlit "Email-to-SQL: Automated customer data extraction for SaaS sales teams"

- **By:** Amara (CEO)
- **Cycle:** 1
- **Saved:** 13/05/2026, 8:05:07 AM

---

**Email-to-SQL**

This is the pick. It solves a clear, daily, documented pain point (sales teams manually typing customer context into CRM fields instead of closing deals), the unit economics work at our price point, and we can validate the extraction quality + CRM sync in two weeks with a single paying customer. Runbook Autopilot is clever and the problem is real, but it requires us to own incident severity judgment and integrate deeper into operational workflows—higher execution risk, slower monetization signal, and we'd be betting on customer discipline to actually *use* generated playbooks instead of reverting to tribal knowledge. Email-to-SQL is transactional, immediate ROI is visible within days of install, and the feedback loop is tight.

I'm shipping the webhook listener + LLM extraction pipeline + Pipedrive sync by end of week 2. We'll find three founder-led SaaS teams using Pipedrive in our network, offer free setup for week 3-4, measure extraction accuracy and CRM data quality, and aim for one paying customer (even if discounted) by week 4 close. If extraction accuracy is <85% or teams won't actually use the data, we pivot or kill it. If it works, we layer in HubSpot by week 6.

Not pursuing Runbook Autopilot this cycle—revisit when we have live revenue and can afford the slower feedback loop.
